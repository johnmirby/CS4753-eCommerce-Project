About Us

Weblytics is a full service, hassle-free, website evaluation and analytics service. Our business model is simple: for a small fee depending on the extent of service you are interested in, Weblytics will provide information on how your website and any associated servers are performing, as well as ensure that your website is meeting HTML standards.

Some features of this service include checking for broken links on the site, response-time statistics on any provided servers, reports on queries from various locations, and checking the site against the current HTML standards. The information is reported in real-time and in-browser, and the only information needed is the website URL and the IP addresses of any servers to be tested.
